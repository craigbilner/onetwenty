tabata

Tabata is a high-intensity interval training (HIIT), and each exercise in a given workout only lasts four minutes. A workout could look a bit like this:
  • Workout hard for 20 seconds
  • Rest for 10 seconds
  • Complete 8 rounds
You push yourself as hard as you can for 20 seconds and rest for 10 seconds. This is one set. You'll complete eight sets of each exercise.
