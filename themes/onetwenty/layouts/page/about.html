How it works

Our programmes target the strengths and weakness of each individuals physical health, through twenty different types of fitness and training styles. These is very important when it comes to functional training as they all have a crossover with everyday life.
Everyone has different and specific fitness goals, so when you train with us, our broad programming will ensure you achieve your targets, no matter your level of fitness or current ability.

tabata

Tabata is a high-intensity interval training (HIIT), and each exercise in a given workout only lasts four minutes. A workout could look a bit like this:
  • Workout hard for 20 seconds
  • Rest for 10 seconds
  • Complete 8 rounds
You push yourself as hard as you can for 20 seconds and rest for 10 seconds. This is one set. You'll complete eight sets of each exercise.

gynmastic

Gymnastics workouts increase your strength and flexibility while boosting muscular endurance. Much like bodyweight training, it is done without the use of free weights or machines, but using your own weight to provide resistance against gravity.
